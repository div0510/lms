An EMDR Trainer is an EMDRIA approved EMDR Consultant who has undergone an extensive application process to become an educator in the field of EMDR therapy. To obtain this credential, the trainer has submitted all related training content and materials to EMDRIA for review, including but not limited LMS platform, trainer content and essay and these materials have been reviewed and meet EMDRIA basic training curriculum requirements and the overall standards set out by EMDRIA for EMDR basic training. To maintain this credential, the EMDR Trainer must continue to satisfy EMDRIA requirements which include but are not limited to participation in Trainer Day events, and adherence to EMDRIA policies and procedures.
